Deleted Message Smart Recovery

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Overview

Deleted Message Smart Recovery App (DMSR) is developed by Satellite World Maps as a Free app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your information. By installing and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

The app focuses on device-based recovery. All scanning, detection, and processing take place within your device unless otherwise stated. We do not collect or upload your personal messages, photos, media, or notification content to any server.

Information App Does Not Collect

  • Personal identity information
  • Contact details
  • Location Information
  • Special Categories of personal data (race, ethnicity, beliefs, sexual orientation, political opinions, trade union membership, health, genetic, criminal data)

Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

To maintain performance and provide technical support, the following technical data may be collected via third-party SDKs:

  • Device model
  • IP address
  • OS version
  • Crash logs
  • Session duration & App usage behavior
  • System details for debugging

Analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Firebase may be used to analyze feature usage. User gallery images, messages, and files remain offline and are never uploaded.

We use trusted third-party services for ads and analytics, including:

Log Data

In case of errors, Log Data may include IP address, device name, OS version, app configuration, time and date of use, and similar statistics.

Data Security

We implement strict security measures, but no electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Limitations of Message Recovery

  • Messages deleted before installing the app cannot be recovered.
  • If notifications are disabled, recovery will not work.
  • Encrypted/hidden media may not be recoverable.

User Rights

  • Request deletion of third-party collected data
  • Limit ad personalization
  • We assist in communicating with providers
