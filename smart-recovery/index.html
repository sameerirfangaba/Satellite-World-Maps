Overview

Deleted Message Smart Recovery App (DMSR) is developed by Satellite World Maps as a Free app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your information. By installing and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

The app focuses on device-based recovery. All scanning, detection, and processing take place within your device unless otherwise stated. We do not collect or upload your personal messages, photos, media, or notification content to any server.

Information App does not Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or transmit the following sensitive personal information:

  • Personal identity information
  • Contact details
  • Location Information
  • Special Categories of personal data (race, ethnicity, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, political opinions, trade union membership, health, genetic, criminal convictions & biometric data)

Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

To maintain performance and provide technical support, below technical information will be collected automatically by third-party SDKs:

  • Device model
  • IP address
  • OS version
  • Crash logs
  • App usage behavior (Features & Session duration for App performance improvement)
  • Basic system details (for debugging)

Analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Firebase may be used to analyze feature usage and improve user experience. User gallery images, messages, any media files are used only within the device and is not uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. Any such data will be removed promptly.

Limitations of Message Recovery

This app recovers deleted messages using notification access only. Certain limitations apply:

  • Messages deleted before installing the app cannot be recovered
  • If notifications are disabled, recovery will not work
  • Encrypted or hidden media may not be fully accessible

Some apps may restrict access due to their security policies.
