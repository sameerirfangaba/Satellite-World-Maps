Satellite World Maps built the Smart Printer app as a free service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application. We do not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy. By installing or using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

By accessing or registering the App, the user agrees to the privacy notice and consents to collecting, processing, transferring, and using your personal data.

Information the App Does Not Collect

Smart Printer is designed to operate with maximum data staying on the user's device. We do not directly collect, store, or transmit the following sensitive personal information:

  • Personal identity information
  • Contact details
  • Location Information
  • Special Categories of personal data (race, ethnicity, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, political opinions, trade union membership, health, genetic & biometric data)
  • Criminal convictions and offenses (not collected)

All processing related to printing remains inside the user's device unless otherwise stated.

Information the App Processes Locally

  • Device and usage information (app version, OS, IP, browser, device identifiers, session info)
  • Selected documents / Gallery images / Photos / Files / Notes for printing
  • Crash Logs & Diagnostics
  • Wi-Fi network information to discover compatible printers

This information is used only within the device and is not uploaded to any server.

Third-Party Service Providers

The app uses third-party services for performance and basic analytics. These may process limited technical data to support ads and analytics.

Service Providers may disclose data only as required by law, to protect rights or safety, or to trusted providers bound by privacy rules.

Children's Privacy

Smart Printer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is safe for general use.

Data Security

Smart Printer uses local processing, reducing privacy risks. Temporary data stays on the device and is not stored after use. We implement physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect information. Use of the Service is at your own risk.
